üé≠ GPT Voice Bridge - Setup Guide

Welcome! This extension lets your GPT characters automatically speak with their unique voices in Zoom meetings. No more copying and pasting!

üìã Quick Setup (5 minutes)

Step 1: Get Your ttsMP3 API Key

  1. Go to ttsMP3.com/register
  2. Purchase a 1-year plan (API access included)
  3. Email support@ttsmp3.com requesting your API key
  4. They'll send you an API key within 24 hours

Step 2: Install Virtual Audio Device

Why? This lets Zoom "hear" the character voices from your browser.

Windows:

  • Download VB-Audio Virtual Cable (free)
  • Install and restart your computer

Mac:

  • Download BlackHole (free)
  • Install and grant audio permissions

Linux:

pactl load-module module-null-sink sink_name=virtual_audio

Step 3: Configure the Extension

  1. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Enter your ttsMP3 API key
  3. Make sure "Auto-Voice Enabled" is ON
  4. Click "Save Settings"
  5. Click "Test Voice" to verify it works

Step 4: Set Up Zoom Audio

  1. Open Zoom settings ‚Üí Audio
  2. Set Microphone to your virtual audio device:
    • Windows: "CABLE Output (VB-Audio Virtual Cable)"
    • Mac: "BlackHole 2ch"
    • Linux: "virtual_audio"
  3. Test with "Test Microphone" - you should see audio levels when characters speak
üí° Pro Tip: Set your system's default audio output to the virtual device, so ALL browser audio (including character voices) goes to Zoom.

üé¨ How to Use

Format Your GPT Responses

In your custom GPT's instructions, tell it to format character dialogue like this:

[CHARACTER: Viki] Well hello there, love! Ready to chat about your story? [CHARACTER: Kira] I've got some sassy insights for you! [CHARACTER: Chuck] Let me protect you from plot holes...

The extension automatically detects these patterns:

  • [CHARACTER: Name] dialogue
  • **Name:** dialogue
  • [Name]: dialogue
  • Name: dialogue (at start of line)

Talk to Your Characters

  1. Start a Zoom meeting (or join one)
  2. Open ChatGPT with your custom GPT
  3. Ask your characters questions
  4. When they respond, their voice automatically plays
  5. Zoom participants hear the characters speaking!
‚ú® That's it! No copying, no pasting. Just talk to your characters and they speak automatically.
